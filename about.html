About

Stark Industries is a multinational industrial company owned by Tony Stark, and is the largest tech conglomerate in the world.

Executive Team

Tony Stark // CEO

Tony Stark is the President and Chief Executive Officer (CEO) at Stark. At the age of 15, Tony entered the electrical engineering program at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) and graduated at the top of his class at age 17. At the age of 21, Tony inherited Stark and ushered in a new era bringing technology into the life of the masses.

Pepper Potts // EVP & CFO

Pepper Potts is Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer (CFO) at Stark, managing the financial strategy and operations of a company with more than 73,000 employees and total revenue for fiscal year 2017 of $48.0 billion. Potts is committed to maximizing long-term shareholder value, ensuring a balanced portfolio of growth initiatives, and maintaining the high level of integrity and transparency for which Stark is known.
